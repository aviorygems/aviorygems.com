NATURAL GEMSTONES

Aviory is one of the largest gemstone dealers in Sri Lanka as well as miners and exporters of precious and semiprecious gemstones. We offer the most beautiful gemstones varying from highest to commercial fine collection of Ceylon Blue Sapphires, yellow sapphire, Star Sapphires, Rubies, pink sapphires, Alexandrite, Padparadscha, and semiprecious gemstones unique, attractive, and high quality.

OUR MISSION

To add value to the people's lives through Ceylon natural gemstones and our designs and by delivering high-quality craftsmanship through our products. Forward the Ceylon natural gems to the International market in international standards.

OUR VISION
